# Privacy Policy for Journal Rounds

**Last Updated:** November 27, 2025

## Introduction

Journal Rounds ("we," "our," or "us") is a mobile application that helps healthcare professionals discover, read, and organize medical research articles from PubMed. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information:** Email address, display name, and authentication credentials
- **Professional Profile:** Role (e.g., Resident/Fellow, MD/DO, PhD), specialty, and institution (optional)
- **Journal Preferences:** Medical journals you follow and reading preferences
- **Subscription Information:** Premium subscription status managed through Apple via RevenueCat

### Usage Data
- **Reading Activity:** Articles you view, bookmark, or save to folders
- **App Interactions:** Features used, time spent reading, search queries
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, app version
- **Performance Data:** Crash reports, error logs, and diagnostic information

### Article Data
- **Bookmarks:** Articles you save for later reading
- **Folders:** Custom organization folders you create
- **Reading History:** Articles you've viewed (optional feature you can disable)
- **AI Usage:** When you request AI-generated summaries of article abstracts

## What We Do NOT Collect

- **Patient information:** We do not collect any patient health information
- **Medical records:** We are not a medical record system
- **Clinical data:** No clinical or diagnostic information is collected
- **Protected Health Information (PHI):** We do not handle PHI

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Deliver personalized feeds of medical research articles based on journals you follow
- Process your subscription and manage your account
- Generate AI-powered summaries of research article abstracts (Premium feature)
- Remember your reading preferences and organize your saved articles
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Send important updates about the app or your subscription
- Provide customer support
- Prevent fraud and ensure security

## Data Storage and Security

- **Cloud Storage:** Your data is securely stored using Supabase (PostgreSQL-based platform)
- **Encryption:** All data transmission uses industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption
- **Access Controls:** Strict authentication and authorization controls protect your data
- **Data Retention:** Your data is retained while your account is active. Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days

- **Purpose:** Source of medical research articles

## Your Data Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction:** Update or correct your profile information within the app
- **Deletion:** Delete your account and all associated data through the app settings
- **Export:** Download your bookmarks and folder data
- **Opt-Out:** Disable reading history tracking in app settings
- **Unsubscribe:** Cancel your Premium subscription at any time

To exercise these rights, use the in-app settings or contact us at: norain.projects@gmail.com

## Children's Privacy

Journal Rounds is intended for healthcare professionals and medical students aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from users under 18. If we discover we have collected information from a user under 18, we will delete it immediately.
