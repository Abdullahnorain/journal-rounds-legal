## Data Source

All medical articles in Journal Rounds come from **PubMed** (https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/), the free database of biomedical literature maintained by the U.S. National Library of Medicine (NLM), part of the National Institutes of Health (NIH).

PubMed contains over 36 million citations from:
- MEDLINE (medical journals)
- Life science journals
- Online books and articles
- Peer-reviewed research

Journal Rounds makes this vast library accessible and easy to navigate on your mobile device.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is Journal Rounds free?**  
A: Yes! Core features are free forever. Premium ($3.99/month) includes unlimited AI summaries.

**Q: Where do articles come from?**  
A: All articles come from PubMed, the public medical literature database maintained by the U.S. National Library of Medicine.

**Q: Can I use Journal Rounds for patient care?**  
A: Journal Rounds is a research tool, not a clinical documentation system. Don't store patient information in the app.

**Q: Are AI summaries accurate?**  
A: AI summaries are generally accurate but should always be verified against the original article before clinical use.

**Q: Can I access full-text articles?**  
A: Journal Rounds provides abstracts from PubMed. For full-text access, you'll need journal subscriptions or institutional access.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**  
A: Manage your subscription in your Apple Account Settings. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your billing period.
